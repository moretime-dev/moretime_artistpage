About me

Hi, my name is Moretime and I am an Indie Pop/Folk Singer and Songwriter from Germany. I write songs about love and life and everything in between. In a world, where it seems hard to find a place to rest sometimes, I want to provide an escape from everyday stress for you. Let me invite you to a dream we can dream together. To a plane in between worlds, where we can find together and exist in peace and happiness.

If you feel weird and out of place in life, looking for somewhere to feel like you belong, you've come to the right place! Let me take you on a spiritual journey where our minds can be one. Close your eyes, listen and leave your body, just for a while. You'll be back, refreshed and strong and maybe with some new food for thought. And hopefully feeling connected and not alone...
